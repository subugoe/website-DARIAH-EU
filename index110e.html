Structure

 

DARIAH shall be built on network of people, expertise, information, knowledge, content, methods, tools and technologies for investigating, exploring and supporting work across the broad spectrum of the digital humanities.

The General Assembly

The General Assembly will be the supreme organ of DARIAH ERIC and shall have full decision-making power. It will consist of the representatives of all Members, with voting rights, and the representatives of the Observers, without voting rights. The representatives should be representatives of the funding agencies.

The Scientific Board

The Scientific Board will be an advisory body, which will provide advice and guidance to the General Assembly on scientific and technical matters.

The Coordination Board

The Coordination Board will be the operational organ of DARIAH ERIC. It will consist of the members of the Board of Directors, all VCC Heads and one delegate appointed by each Member and each Observer.

The Board of Directors

The Board of Directors will be the executive organ and the legal representative of DARIAH. It will be composed of three Directors, appointed by the General Assembly.

The members of the Board of Directors in the Construction Phase shall be

  1. Laurent Romary (Director of the Transition Phase), CNRS, France
  2. Tobias Blanke, GCDH, Germany
  3. The third director will be appointed in due course

The DARIAH-EU Coordination Office

The DARIAH-EU Coordination Office (DCO) is a distributed unit, which is responsible for the coordination of the activities of DARIAH at the European Level.  It supports and integrates all levels of DARIAH ERIC (General Assembly, Scientific Board, Board of Directors, Coordination Board and the Virtual Competency Centres). In its role as a coordinator, the DCO oversees the interactions with all DARIAH ERIC partners and boards and takes on a variety of vertical tasks (e.g. controlling administrative procedures) and horizontal tasks (e.g. central services, overall financing, legal and tax requirements, transfer of skills and knowledge).
The DARIAH-EU Coordination office (DCO) is a virtual organisation, based in three countries: France with the main responsibility of all budgetary and legal coordination, Germany with the focus on the overall coordination and communication and The Netherlands focussing on coordinating DARIAH-EU’s Virtual Competency Centres (VCCs).

Members of the DCO are:

  1. Henk Harmsen, Chief Integration Officer (CIO)
  2. Sally Chambers, Secretary General (SG)
  3. Gabriele Kraft, Communication Officer (CO)

 

DARIAH enters the Transition Phase

Following the successful completion of the DARIAH preparatory phase project in February 2011, DARIAH has moved into the transition phase. During the transition phase, DARIAH will submit an application to the European Commission to establish a European Research Infrastructure Consortium (ERIC). This legal framework will facilitate the long-term sustainability of DARIAH.
