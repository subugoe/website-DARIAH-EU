Work package 5 – Governance and logistic work

The general objective is to deliver a business plan that describes strategic choices, the organisational set-up, financial possibilities and the operational plan for the construction phase of DARIAH. There are three main activities:

1) Organisational set-up:
The results from the strategic and financial work will be used to determine:
• The management structure, including roles and responsibilities of the management, steering committee and advisory board,
• The role of stakeholders in the organisational structure,
• Staff requirements and job descriptions,
• The location of the central office and practical needs,
• The role and functioning of a network of experts and the profile of such experts,
• The relationship with national data centres and research communities, and
• A rationale and procedures for the accession of new partners.

2) Operational plan:
The recommendation of the strategic and technical teams will be integrated into an operational plan that describes:
• All conceptual, technical and other work needed during the construction phase,
• Procedures for contingency management and quality assurance,
• The communication strategy and operational plan for communication activities during the construction phase,
• The transfer of knowledge, training and dissemination activities, and
• The timing of all tasks in the construction phase.

3) Business plan:
The business plan brings together results of the communication, strategic, financial, legal and technical work packages, as well as the organisational set-up and operational plan.
The business plan will provide:
• A general overview of the current situation,
• Short- and long-term objectives and strategic choices,
• A detailed description of the actual organisational set-up,
• A detailed operational plan including a description and timing of activities and workload for the construction phase, and
• A financial plan for the construction and operational phases.
