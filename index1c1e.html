Case Study of Slovenia - 7. National ESFRI Roadmap

 
The National Esfri Roadmap is currently under preparation. In addition to Dariah (Digital Research Infrastructure for the Arts and Humanities), there are also coordination groups for the Clarin network (which does not enjoy any financial support in the preliminary stages) and Share (Upgrade of the Survey of Health, Ageing and Retirement in Europe).

Currently, we are in the stage of presenting programmes and projects and searching for national support for further Dariah development at the national level.
