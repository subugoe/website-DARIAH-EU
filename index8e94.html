IRCHSS - Irish Research Council for the Humanities and Social Sciences

 

The Irish Research Council for the Humanities and Social Sciences (IRCHSS) was established in 2000 by the Minister for Education and Science and is a member of the European Science Foundation. Its mission is to:
• encourage excellence and the highest standards in the humanities and social sciences,
• encourage co-operation between the humanities and social sciences,
• develop opportunities and policies for the promotion of research,
• support research and promote interdisciplinary collaboration,
• facilitate communication and research networks,
• promote cross-border co-operation on the island, and
• maximise research funding.

The Irish Research Council for the Humanities and Social Sciences (IRCHSS) currently operates six national research schemes from postgraduate to senior collaborative project level. It promotes international research collaboration through its role as the national agency for the Humanities and Social Sciences in the European Framework Programme (FP7) and the European Research Areas NETworks HERA and NORFACE.
