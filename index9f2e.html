KCL DDH: 3 research developers

Wednesday, 05 September 2012  

The Department of Digital Humanities (DDH) is looking for a number of skilled developers to join its Research and Development team. These posts all involve implementation and functional design work (in collaboration with other members of the R&D team and external partners) across two - three varied and challenging research projects.

The closing date is 13th September 2012 with interviews to be held during the week beginning 17th September 2012.
