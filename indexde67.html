DIGHUMLAB Launch

Thursday, 09 August 2012  
DIGHUMLAB (Digital Humanities Laboratory, Denmark) launches on Monday 10 September 2012.
We invite all members of DARIAH, attendance is free but online registration is required as seats are limited.
 
Details: Monday 10 September 2012: 12.00 – 17.30
Location: Peter Bøgh Andersen Auditorium, Nygaard building, on the corner of Finlandsgade og Helsingforsgade, Aarhus, Denmark.
 
Speakers include:
  • Rector Lauritz B. Holm-Nielsen & Dean of Arts, Mette Thunø, Aarhus University
  • Danish Minister for Science, Innovation and Higher Education, Morten Østergaard
  • The DIGHUMLAB research theme leaders
  • Sally Chambers, Secretary General, DARIAH-EU Coordination Office
  • Steven Krauwer, CLARIN ERIC Executive Director
  • Professor Patrik Svensson, HUMlab, Umeå University
  • Professor Lorna Hughes, University of Wales
  • Associate Professor Palmyre Pierroux, InterMedia, University of Oslo
  • Professor Lily Díaz-Kommonen, Media Lab, Aalto University
For further information please visit the website: http://dighumlab.dk/index.php?id=2146
