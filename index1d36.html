DARIAH at London Book Fair 2012

Sunday, 04 March 2012  

"The (digital) Future of Academic publishing? Digital Research Infrastructures for the Humanities: The DARIAH Project" is the title of the session with which DARIAH takes part in the London Book Fair 2012. Sheila Anderson, Director of the Centre of eResearch, Department of Digital Humanities at King’s College London, and Tobias Blanke, Senior Lecturer in the Centre for e-Research and one of the directors of DARIAH, et al. will discuss about DARIAH which is seen as a cutting-edge – and in many ways controversial – initiative by the publishing ‘establishment’.
