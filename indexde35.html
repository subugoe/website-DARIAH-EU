DARIAH leaders will meet in Bruxelles, 26 Nov 2007

Thursday, 01 November 2007  

The leaders of DARIAH are going to have a meeting in Bruxelles, Belgium, on November 26 2007. EU project officer Lorenza Saracco will be present to discuss aspects of the Preparing DARIAH proposal and changes in the FP7 framework.

EU project officer Lorenza Saracco will update leaders of the different work packages and key members be on the evaluation of the Preparing DARIAH proposal. She summarise the latest developments of the FP7 framework and the negotiations of ESFRI projects. Later on there will be time to discuss issues of the Preparing DARIAH proposal with her. Communications activities are also on the agenda.
