Case Study of Ireland

 

Abstract

This is an overview of the development of the digital humanities infrastructure in the Republic of Ireland. The aim of the case study is to identify the development of a digital humanities infrastructure and to map all the relevant recommendations, strategies and initiatives put forward to enable the development of future policies. It also identifies the key policy makers and planning processes and accounts for achievement and under-achievement to date. It will address the challenges to the future development of a digital humanities infrastructure in Ireland.
