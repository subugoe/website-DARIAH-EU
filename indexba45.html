IRCHSS DARIAH Lecture: Digital Humanities – New Frontiers

Wednesday, 30 September 2009  

Digital Humanities - New Frontiers

Wednesday, 14 October 2009, 14:00
Anatomy Lecture Theatre, Anatomy Building, Trinity College Dublin

As part of the IRCHSS 10 Celebrations and Dublin Innovation week Trinity College Dublin is hosting the IRCHSS DARIAH Lecture: 'Digital Humanities – New Frontiers'

Professor Tony Hey, Corporate Vice-President for Research, Microsoft, will deliver a keynote address on digital humanities. This will be followed by a panel discussion with Professor Martin Curley, Director, Intel Labs Europe and Dr. Marie Wallace, Senior Development Manager, IBM. In addition, there will be presentations and a poster session on digital humanities projects currently taking place in the higher-education sector in Ireland. We hope to broadcast the event in Second Life.
