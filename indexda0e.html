AA - Academy of Athens

 

The Academy of Athens is the oldest national research institution for the sciences and humanities in Greece. It promotes learning and expertise by conducting research projects and by collecting and disseminating resources and research results.

Developing a digital resource collection and a digital research infrastructure are among its current priorities.

Role in DARIAH
The Academy of Athens’ role in the “Preparing DARIAH” project consists in its participation in the strategic and financial work.
