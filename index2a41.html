Advisory Board

 

The DARIAH Advisory Board is appointed by the Management Board and provides advice in substantive scientific and methodological issues. The advisory board includes representatives of all stakeholder groups: researcher communities, data providers, funding bodies, information/grid technology institutes, cultural heritage institutions, ICT facilities providers.
