DARIAH General VCC meeting

Thursday, 29 March 2012  

We are  happy to announce that  almost 80 DARIAH friends from 14 countries confirmed their participation in the meeting by know.

The DARIAH General VCC meeting will take place in Utrecht in the Netherlands on 2/3 April 2012. We invite all DARIAH members to take part in this collaborative event which will include a mixture of plenary and parallel VCC sessions.

We will meet in the Stadskasteel Oudaen
