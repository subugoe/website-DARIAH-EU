Information event in Brussels on 18 April 2008 for call for proposals FP7 framework

Thursday, 20 March 2008  
The European Commission is organising an information event on the fourth call for proposals under the e-Infrastructures topic of the FP7 "Capacities" Specific Programme. It will be held on 18 April 2008 in Brussels.

The event will focus on the following topics of the second call:

• INFRA-2007-1.2.1: GÉANT

• INFRA-2007-1.2.2: Scientific Data Infrastructures

The agenda and further details on this event can be found here. The participation is free of charge, but please register as soon as possible, as places are limited. The registration form is here.
