DARIAH-ERIC: step 1 of submission successfully completed

Thursday, 18 October 2012  
We are very pleased to announce that an important step towards establishing DARIAH as a European organisation has been achieved.  

France, the host country of the future organisation, the DARIAH ERIC (European Research Infrastructure Consortium), has submitted the 'Step 1' application to the European Commission.  

The Commission, with the assistance of a group of experts, will assess this initial application for compliance with the ERIC legal regulations.  The results of this evaluation are expected in late November / early December.  

Following a successful evaluation, DARIAH will be invited to submit a formal request, the 'Step 2 application', to establish the European organisation.
