UGOE - University of Goettingen (Goettingen State and University Library)

 

The Goettingen State and University Library (UGOE) is one of the largest scientific university libraries in Germany and assumes various roles in information management and the development of digital library services. It tasks include:
• institutional repositories
• digital long-term preservation / data curation
• digitization/retro-digitization
• technology infrastructures for the humanities (grid, web services, metadata)
• international standardisation (PREMIS, METS, TEI, DC)

Goettingen State and University Library works towards a national e-humanities infrastructure for Germany, together with the Max Planck Digital Library and other national partners. In close cooperation with Goettingen University and national and international partners, the library provides an institutional infrastructure for research and education.

Role in Preparing DARIAH project
The Goettingen State and University Library's main focus is in DARIAHs technical work packages. It leads the conceptual modeling work package and contributes expertise in grid technologies and their integration with information management. The library also participates in the governance and logistical work package.
