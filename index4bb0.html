Trinity College in Dublin offers a Part Time Research Assistant Position

Friday, 19 August 2011  

The Trinity College in Dublin offers a Part Time Research Assistant Position in the Virtual Competency Centre (VCC) 2 of the DARIAH project

A part time position is available at the Long Room Hub as the Research Assistant to support the Irish contribution to the European Digital Research Infrastructure for the Arts and Humanities, (DARIAH). This is a two year part time contract.

Position Requirements:
Essential
Experience working in an administrative role within a university or not for profit organisation; excellent organizational skills; experience with budgets and record keeping; experience organizing summer schools, workshops, and symposia; experience working within a digital humanities or digital library context.

Desired
Experience working within a European context on grant-funded projects; acting in a supporting role in submitting grants (Irish and/or European).

For consideration, please send a full CV and a cover letter to Dr Susan Schreibman by 5.00 25 August 2011.
